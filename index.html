ORCID Display

Display ORCID profiles and publications as beautiful, embeddable cards

Try Another ORCID

Usage

Add ORCID Display to any webpage with just two lines of code:

<script src="https://bbdaniels.github.io/orcid-display/orcid-display.js"></script>
<orcid-profile orcid="0000-0001-9652-6653"></orcid-profile>

Replace the ORCID ID with your own or any researcher's ORCID.
